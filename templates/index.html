What is Spinwords?

Spinwords is a website designed to provide new words every time you click the Spin button.
You can also search for the specific word and show its definitions below.

It's important to learn new vocabulary sometimes. That's why I first created this website to practice myself, but then, I decided to publish it so everyone can use it to learn.

How does it work?

You can find the public repository of Spinwords on GitHub. It's an open source website.
It was made with Python, a popular programming language.
